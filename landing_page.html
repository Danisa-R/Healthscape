About

Mental health can affect healthy relationships, physical health and daily wellbeing. With Healthscape, we help eliminate the awkward and time-consuming task of finding a therapist, sponsor or someone to hear you out. For individuals with busy schedules and tight wallets, Healthscape is a safe space where anyone can log on and talk to someone about their problems. HealthScape provides an option to have everything in one place, from games to journal to therapist. HealthScape - a place for all your mental health needs.
Healthscape allows you to talk through your troubles at the click of a button, anywhere around the world.
With open arms and acceptance, finding a trusted confidant has never been so easy
